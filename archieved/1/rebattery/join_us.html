Be part of a movement that makes a difference! Our Re-Battery Recycling Program welcomes collaborative contributions to support our planet and advance battery repurposing research. Here are ways to get involved:
Become a Supporter
- Fund our project: Help us continue our work by contributing through our GoFundMe. Every donation counts, and we appreciate your support. For larger contributions, please contact our team members directly.
- Recognition: We value every donor and would like to acknowledge your contribution. Your support helps us move forward with our mission.

Stay Updated: Stay informed about our progress and upcoming projects by leaving your email address below. This is a great way to stay connected and learn about our initiatives.

Join Our Team
If you're passionate about environmental initiatives and want to work with us to increasing used battery collections, we invite you to join us! Our team is also focused on researching innovative ways to repurpose and recycle battery materials. Simply email our project leader with a brief introduction. It would be great if you could include your resume, interests, and any projects you're passionate about. Don't worry about formalities; it's not an internship application!

Collaborate with Us: Are you an organization or initiative interested in collaborating? Or perhaps you're a visiting member with questions or ideas to share? Reach out to any of our team members to discuss potential partnerships and learn more about our work!
